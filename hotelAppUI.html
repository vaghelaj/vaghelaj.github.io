Hotel App

A concept iOS hotel booking app. For this project, I challenged myself to design a hotel booking process in 24-hours. So I focused on the hotel information, hotel suites availability and hotel payment screens.

For the design, I wanted to use a bold primary colour and a simple sans-serif font while keeping the overall layout quite simple.
